About Us

How we work is changing.

We are here to help close your skills gap. Put simply we help you move beyond buying skills to building skills. Getting the right people, adapting the right skills, aligned to business needs.

It should be the easiest thing in the world to understand and grow skills right across your workforce. But it isn’t, right? We know. We’ve been there.

We built Yfor because we understand that people and skills don’t fit neatly into little boxes. Think of Yfor as your skills intelligence system.

The complete picture. In real time. At your fingertips. At last.

Some questions?

Which plan should I choose?

Yfor has 3 simple rates dependent on workforce size. All features are included.

Do I need to define all my skills needs?

Yfor’s skills map gets you started - just edit if necessary.

Can I use it for my Contractors & Freelancers?

Absolutely, you define your users & simply provide access.

How do we define our OKRs?

See the guide below, keep it simple, share ownership & feedback loops continuous.

Can I store my certificates digitally?

Yes, simply apply your QR code and scan & save. Add expiry date & Yfor will even remind you.

Can my mandatory certificates be validated remotely?

Yfors digital learning wallet enables your Employees to access all their certificates from their own personal dashboard.

Can I promote Internal vacancies?

Yfor enables you to promote to all Employees, to skill match & message best fit employees.

How can I apply for internal vacancies?

Employee Dashboard you can build your skills map, set career goals and get notified directly of all internal vacancies.
